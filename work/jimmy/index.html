PROJECT

2020 POSTCARD

Taiwan attraction | Jimmy park in Yilan

台灣景點 | 宜蘭吉米公園

DATE

2019 December 30th

CLIENT

木石二文創

DESIGNER

Weijiun

PROMOTION

Vending machine in Jimmy Park

Jimmy Park is located next to Yilan Station. The installation art made from the picture book by a well-known picture book writer Jimmy in Taiwan.

The postcard was inspired by the sculpture in Jimmy Park which is an iconic work named "Heading left, heading right." My design was chosen to sale in Jimmy Park by vending machine.

吉米公園位於宜蘭火車站旁，公園內的裝置藝術皆由台灣知名插畫家吉米繪製。

此明信片設計視覺源自公園內非常代表性的雕塑藝術作品「向左走，向右走」，此設計也被選為2020年公園明信片並於園內販賣機販售。
