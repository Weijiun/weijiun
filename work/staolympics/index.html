PROJECT

Creative Olympics

Star to Asia｜Corporate Envelpe and bottled water

亞星通｜創意奧林匹克｜企業信封袋及迎賓瓶裝水

DATE

November 20th, 2020

COMPETITION

Star to Asia | 亞星通股份有限公司
Gold Medal | 金賞

DESIGNER

Weijiun

⊘Corporate Identity 【Creative Olympics】：
The competition invited every employee to design new graphic for corporate envelope (in potrait and landscape sizes) + bottled water which use to welcome guest in conference.

My design concept combined with company's logo and three symbols which are expect to shape business identity.
• Layers → Knowledge
• Star → Passion
• Dimension → Logic

I am honoured to recieve this gold medal award and my visual assets was able to print on company's requirements.

⊘企業識別應用【奧林匹克創意大賽】：
募集內部設計師製作企業信封袋(直版&橫板)＋會議迎賓用瓶裝水。
此版視覺延伸現企業Logo流星意象為基礎，再發展3個核心元素。
• Layers → Knowledge (層層關卡 → 知識疊加)
• Star → Passion (星星 → 熱忱)
• Dimension → Logic (立體建構 → 清晰邏輯)
期許將3個延伸意涵融入企業文化中。

此設計獲 Star to Asia 【奧林匹克創意大賽】 ☆金賞☆
且應用於現企業用信封及瓶裝水。
