PROJECT

Charlesphy

Charlesphy｜CIS｜Brand Design

茶室啡｜品牌視覺設計

DATE

Oct 18th, 2022

CLIENT

Charlesphy｜茶室啡

DESIGNER

Weijiun

The geographical condition in Taiwan has advantage of tea plantation with great soil and irrigation system. Charlesphy is a new brand launched by ZHUANG - Turkey rice which is a well known resturant in Taichung. Charlesphy offers quality Taiwanese tea and coffee.

The logo consists of 4C standing for Cups of Coffee and Cha from Charlesphy and the 4 pieces of tea leaves in the middle. Every elements is important for Charlesphy to make a cup of tea and coffee as if its logo connects and combines with 4C and 4 leaves.

茶室啡是莊家火雞肉飯推出的新品牌。 台灣有得天獨厚的條件，孕育好茶、好水、好味道， 一起迎接全新的「茶室啡」吧！

C字母為主要形體，意涵CHA、COFFEE，與商品茶飲、茶杯結合，組成的Charlesphy。

中間由4片茶葉對稱，表現好茶葉、好味道，由4個C環環相扣，只提供好品質的茶。
