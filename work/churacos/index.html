PROJECT

Skincare Package

Churacos｜Package design｜All in One Gel

俏樂斯｜包裝設計｜全方位保濕水凝霜

DATE

May 10th, 2022

CLIENT

Churacos｜俏樂斯

DESIGNER

Weijiun

DIRECTOR

Teresa

● Package design：
It's a collaboration with Overseas department to redesign the package for Churacos skincare products (All in One Gel) and sale in Hong Kong. The main features of this series is its ingredient from Okinawa in Japan. Therefore, some elements such as ocean and sea ​​turtles from its logo can be recognised pattern on the package. Considering display in cosmetic shop with other similar products, it's better to use pretty bright colour to stand out among those other choice and it's also connecting with Okinawa's vibe.

● 3 colours：
❍ Green｜Warabihada All in One Gel
❍ Pink｜Churakanasa All in One Gel
❍ Blue｜Neo Warabihada All in One Gel

● 包裝設計：
此專案為跨部門合作案件(海外事業部門)， 為日本俏樂斯客戶的全方位保濕水凝霜重新設計包裝於香港販售。 商品特點為來自沖繩的天然成分，因此以其logo的元素(海洋、海龜等)及商品亮眼的用色來凸顯來自活潑、熱帶的 沖繩保養品。

● 3款顏色：
❍ 綠｜全方位保濕水凝霜
❍ 粉｜靚白抗皺水凝凍
❍ 藍｜超導逆時彈力水凝霜
