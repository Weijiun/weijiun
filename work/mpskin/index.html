Skin Counsultation

One of the most important competitions I took part in university was a conceptual design for the German Red Dot Award. I created a watch called ‘iRemedy’ to solve the problem of forgetting to take medicine. This product helps the elderly or patients with long-term requirements for taking medicine by removing the need to keep track of time.

It combined functions such as impulse measurement, abnormal notification, a red light reminder and pill compartments. My submission was honoured by being shortlisted, a precious experience that had a positive impact on my life.

為美樂思保養品牌規劃「肌膚檢測單元」，幫助顧客了解自己的肌膚狀態，並選用適合的保養方式。為其討論及設計 5道基礎問題， 透過簡單互動方式讓顧客完成小測驗，也為品牌增加主推商品的曝光度。
